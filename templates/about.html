What We Offer

Our platform is designed to empower artisans and provide a trustworthy, enjoyable shopping experience for consumers. Key features include:

  • Product Catalogs
  • Personal Artisan Narratives (Shop Inspiring Stories)
  • Category and Location Searching Filters
  • Safe and Secure Payment Methods
  • Post-Purchase Reviews for artisan stores

Likharyo is committed to driving positive social and economic change in the Philippines. We specifically contribute to two critical UN Sustainable Development Goals:

SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth

By providing local artists with a digital storefront, Likharyo directly supports artisans by giving them access to a wider market. Each purchase guarantees just compensation and sustains a traditional craft, promoting full and productive employment for Filipino creators.

SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

We encourage consumers to prefer locally manufactured goods. Our site uses shop narratives and product tags to highlight sustainable practices, such as the use of recycled materials or conventional methods, promoting conscious and sustainable consumption patterns.
